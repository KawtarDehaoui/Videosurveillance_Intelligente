About Us


This Web application has been designed as part of a Final Year Project to obtain a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, Networks, and Multimedia (IRM) at the Faculty of Sciences and Techniques of Mohammedia.

Supervised by:

  • Prof. LEGHRIS Cherkaoui

It is an application equipped with an intelligent video surveillance system specialized in ensuring security in a parking area.


The application offers the following features:

  1. Checking the availability of parking spaces : Checking the availability of parking spaces: The system allows security agents to monitor and verify the availability of parking spaces in real-time. They can quickly identify and assign vacant spaces to incoming vehicles, ensuring efficient parking management.
  2. Displaying alerts in case of incidents : Displaying alerts in case of incidents: The system is equipped with video surveillance technology that enables security agents to receive real-time alerts in case of incidents or security breaches within the parking area. This ensures a prompt response to maintain the safety and security of the premises.
  3. Detecting license plate numbers of cars : Detecting license plate numbers of cars: By utilizing advanced ANPR (Automatic Number Plate Recognition) technology, the system enables security agents to automatically capture and record license plate numbers of vehicles entering or exiting the parking area. This helps in tracking and identifying vehicles for security and law enforcement purposes.
